About PowellTech

Here at PowellTech, you're more than a repair number. We value and appreciate you as a customer. Vision

Our vision at PowellTech is to ensure that you receive a timely, thorough, and effective repair to keep your technology in your hands. We are quickly evolving into a world solely reliant upon technology, we simply want to keep you functioning.
